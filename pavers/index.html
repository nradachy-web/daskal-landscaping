Paver Installation

Interlocking pavers for patios, driveways, and walkways — precision-installed with proper base work for decades of durability.

Get Your Free Estimate Call (248) 719-0001

Why Pavers?

Pavers are the gold standard for outdoor surfaces. Unlike poured concrete that cracks, or asphalt that deteriorates, interlocking pavers flex with Michigan's freeze-thaw cycles, drain properly, and can be individually replaced if ever damaged. They look better, last longer, and add serious value to your home.

Paver Applications

✓

Patios

Custom paver patios in endless patterns — herringbone, running bond, basketweave, circular — with borders and accents.

✓

Driveways

Heavy-duty interlocking pavers rated for vehicle traffic. Transform your driveway from boring concrete to a statement feature.

✓

Walkways

Front entry walks, garden paths, and side yard connections — safe, level, and beautiful.

✓

Pool Decks

Slip-resistant pavers designed for pool surrounds — cool underfoot and properly graded for drainage.

Our Installation Process

  • Excavation to proper depth (8-12" depending on application)
  • Compacted gravel base for stability and drainage
  • Leveling sand bed screeded to precise grade
  • Pavers laid by hand in your chosen pattern
  • Edge restraints installed to prevent shifting
  • Polymeric sand swept and activated
  • Final compaction and cleanup
