Your Vision, Professionally Designed

Great landscaping starts with great design. At Daskal Landscaping, we don't just show up and start planting — we sit down with you, walk your property, understand how you use your outdoor space, and create a detailed plan that brings your vision to life.

Whether you're starting from a blank slate or refreshing an existing landscape, our design process ensures every plant, every stone, and every feature is intentional — creating a cohesive outdoor environment that increases your home's value and your enjoyment of it.

Our Design Process

1

On-Site Consultation

We walk your property together, discuss your goals, take measurements, and understand how you want to use your outdoor space.

2

Concept & Design

We create a detailed landscape plan with plant selections, hardscape layouts, grading, and drainage — tailored to Michigan's soil and climate.

3

Review & Refine

You review the design, we make adjustments, and finalize materials, timeline, and budget. No surprises.

4

Professional Installation

Our crew brings the plan to life — on time, on budget, and exactly as designed.

What We Design

  • Complete front and backyard landscape plans
  • Planting schemes with native and ornamental species
  • Hardscape integration — patios, walkways, walls
  • Drainage and grading solutions
  • Outdoor living spaces and entertainment areas
  • Seasonal color planning for year-round beauty
  • Low-maintenance and sustainable designs
