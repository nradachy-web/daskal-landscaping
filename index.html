About Daskal

Built on Hard Work & Honest Service

Founded in 2020 by Max Daskal, Daskal Landscaping started with a simple idea: do great work, treat people right, and let the results speak for themselves.

FAQ

Common Questions

How much does a landscaping project cost?

It depends on scope — a simple planting refresh costs far less than a full hardscape or pool installation. We provide free on-site estimates with clear, itemized pricing so you know exactly what to expect.

Do you handle both design and installation?

Yes. We take your project from initial concept through design, material selection, and full installation. You get one team from start to finish.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Northville, Novi, Plymouth, South Lyon, Birmingham, West Bloomfield, Royal Oak, Bloomfield Hills, and surrounding communities in Southeast Michigan.

Do you offer snow removal?

Yes. We provide reliable residential and commercial snow removal throughout the winter season with prompt service after every storm. We use snowblowers and shoveling to protect your surfaces.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Daskal Landscaping is fully licensed and insured in the state of Michigan.
